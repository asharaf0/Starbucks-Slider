Caffe Latte, a new product

$ 20

A Caffè Latte is a creamy and smooth coffee beverage made with a balance of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. The name "latte" means "milk" in Italian, highlighting the drink's emphasis on milk's richness over the espresso's strength.

Strawberry mocha, a new product

$ 20

A strawberry mocha combines the rich, velvety taste of chocolate coffee with the fruity sweetness of strawberries. Imagine a deep brown coffee or espresso base mixed with creamy milk and a touch of chocolate syrup. Then, it’s layered or swirled with a sweet strawberry sauce, giving it a pinkish-red hue against the mocha base.

Doppio espresso, a new product

$ 20

A doppio espresso, or simply "doppio," is a double shot of espresso made by pulling twice the amount of water through a single dose of finely-ground coffee. The result is a stronger, richer, and more concentrated coffee than a standard single espresso.

Matcha latte macchiato, a new product

$ 20

A Matcha Latte Macchiato is a beautifully layered drink that combines the smooth creaminess of steamed milk, the deep, earthy flavor of matcha green tea, and a delicate foam top.
